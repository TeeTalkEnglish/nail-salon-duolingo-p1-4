👩‍🏫 Teacher Mode

Role Play Script

Lesson 4 · Walk-In Client · Star Nails, Pennsauken NJ

👩‍🏫 How to use this page:
  • You (Teacher) read the 🔵 BLUE lines — you play the CLIENT
  • Student says the 🩷 PINK lines — they play NAIL TECH
  • Tap the pink lines to reveal the answer if student is stuck
  • Give the student time to speak before revealing!

📋 Conversation Script

💅 Nail Tech — STUDENT speaks Step 1
"Hello! Do you have an appointment?"
👆 Tap to reveal if student is stuck
💡 Tip: Student should greet AND ask about appointment in one sentence. Listen for: "Hello" + "appointment"
👩 Client Linda — YOU say this Step 2
"No, I don't. Can I walk in?"
💅 Nail Tech — STUDENT speaks Step 3
"Yes, of course! Which service would you like?"
👆 Tap to reveal if student is stuck
💡 Tip: Student should say YES positively then ask about service. Listen for: "of course" + "service"
👩 Client Linda — YOU say this Step 4
"What do you have?"
💅 Nail Tech — STUDENT speaks Step 5
"We have a basic pedicure for 30 minutes, a spa pedicure for 40 minutes, and a deluxe pedicure for 50 minutes."
👆 Tap to reveal if student is stuck
💡 Tip: This is the hardest line! Student must name all 3 services with times. If struggling, let them use notes first. Key words: "basic / spa / deluxe" + "30 / 40 / 50 minutes"
👩 Client Linda — YOU say this Step 6
"I would like the spa pedicure, please."
💅 Nail Tech — STUDENT speaks Step 7
"Perfect! Please go to chair number 2."
👆 Tap to reveal if student is stuck
💡 Tip: Student should confirm the choice positively then direct to a chair. Listen for: "Perfect" + "chair number"
