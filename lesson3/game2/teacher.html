👩‍🏫 Teacher Guide: Small Talk

📋 Guide

📋 How to Use This Game

  1. You play the Customer. Read the customer lines out loud.
  2. The student plays the Nail Tech. They answer on screen.
  3. The game has 10 rounds covering greetings, small talk, and family.
  4. After each round, the Word Help shows the vocabulary.
  5. At the end, the student sees the full conversation to review.

💬 Your Lines (Read These Out Loud)

Round 1: (Customer walks in) — Just wait for student to greet.

Round 2: Say: "Thank you." → Wait for student to introduce themselves.

Round 3: Say: "Nice to meet you too. My name is Lisa." → Wait for student to ask about first time.

Round 4A (YES): Say: "Yes, it is my first time." ✅
→ Student should say: "Welcome! We're happy to have you!"

Round 4B (NO): Say: "No, I came here before." ❌
→ Student should say: "Welcome back! Nice to see you again!"

Round 5: Just wait for student to ask about appointment. Say: "Yes, I do."

Round 6: Wait for student to ask if married. Say: "Yes, I am."

Round 7: Wait for student to show picture. Say: "Oh, she is very cute!"

Round 8: Wait for student to ask about kids. Say: "Yes, I have two kids."

Round 9: Wait for student to ask ages. Say: "My son is 10 and my daughter is 7."

Round 10: Wait for student to ask about school. Say: "Yes, both of them are in school."

Round 11: Say: "It was nice talking to you!" → Wait for student to respond.

Round 12: Pretend to sneeze: "Ah-choo!" 🤧 → Student should say "Bless you!"

📖 Key Vocabulary

Welcome Chào mừng Hi! Welcome to the salon.
Nice to meet you Rất vui được gặp bạn My name is Tee. Nice to meet you!
first time lần đầu tiên Is this your first time here?
Welcome back Chào mừng trở lại Welcome back! Nice to see you again!
appointment lịch hẹn Do you have an appointment?
married kết hôn Are you married?
kids con cái Do you have kids?
How old Bao nhiêu tuổi How old are your kids?
school trường học Are they going to school?
Bless you Chúc sức khỏe Said when someone sneezes.
