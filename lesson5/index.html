💬 Example Sentences
Example 1 – Describing

"I recommend long almond nails in lavender with a chrome finish. Very trendy!"

GUIDE
📖 How to Play
  1. Open Teacher page on one device (or browser tab).
  2. Open Student page on another device (or browser tab).
  3. Teacher shows a nail design and reads the prompt out loud.
  4. Student describes the nails using English vocabulary.
  5. Teacher grades the response: Perfect ✅, Good 👍, or Try Again 🔄.
  6. Play through all 8 rounds and see your final score!
📝 Sentence Patterns
Pattern 1 – Describe
"These are [length] [shape] nails with a [finish] [color] finish."
Pattern 2 – Recommend
"I recommend [length] [shape] nails in [color] with a [finish] finish."
Pattern 3 – Ask
"Would you like [shape] nails in [color]? [finish] or [finish]?"
